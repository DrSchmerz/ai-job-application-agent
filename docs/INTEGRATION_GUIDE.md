## 🚀 The Complete Workflow

### **Step 1: Find Jobs** 🔍

#### Option A: Search Multiple Job Boards
1. Go to **🚀 Workflow** page
2. Click **"Find Jobs"** tab
3. Enter role (e.g., "Solutions Engineer")
4. Select location (Europe, Remote, etc.)
5. Click **"Generate Job Board Links"**
6. Get instant links to:
   - LinkedIn Jobs
   - Wellfound (AngelList)
   - Indeed
   - Glassdoor
   - Remote.co
   - WeWorkRemotely

#### Option B: Import from URL
1. Find a job on any job board
2. Copy the URL
3. Paste in **"Import Job from URL"** section
4. Click **"Import Job Details"**
5. Job description automatically extracted!

#### Option C: Search Target Companies
1. Add companies to **🎯 Target Companies**
2. Click search button next to each company
3. Get direct links to their job boards

---

### **Step 2: Analyze & Apply** ✨

#### From Workflow Page:
1. After importing job, click **"Add to Applications"**
2. Go to **"Quick Apply"** tab
3. Click **"Analyze & Generate Application"**

#### From New Application Page:
1. Go to **✨ New Application**
2. Job details auto-filled if imported
3. Click **"Analyze Job Fit"** → Get AI score
4. Click **"Generate Cover Letter"** → AI writes it
5. Click **"Save Application"** → Done!
